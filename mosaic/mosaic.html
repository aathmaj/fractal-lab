The Burning Ship Fractal

About the Burning Ship Fractal

The Burning Ship fractal is created by iterating the function $z_{n+1} = (|\text{Re}(z_n)| + i|\text{Im}(z_n)|)^2 + c$ in the complex plane. Unlike the Mandelbrot Set, the absolute values of the real and imaginary parts of $z$ are taken at each step. This subtle change in the formula drastically alters the geometry of the resulting fractal, creating a shape that resembles a ship with two burning masts.
